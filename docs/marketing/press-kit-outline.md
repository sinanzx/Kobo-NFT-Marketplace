## FAQ

**Q: What makes K≈çbo different from other NFT platforms?**  
A: K≈çbo uniquely combines multi-modal AI generation, automated copyright compliance, remix capabilities, and DAO governance in one platform. We're the only solution offering legal pre-check scanning and immutable provenance tracking for AI-generated NFTs.

**Q: Which AI engines does K≈çbo support?**  
A: We integrate DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion, Midjourney (images), Runway ML, Pika Labs (video), ElevenLabs (voice), and Suno AI (music), with more engines added based on community votes.

**Q: How does copyright compliance work?**  
A: Every generation undergoes automated pre-check scanning against restricted content blocklists and third-party copyright APIs. Users accept engine-specific TOS before minting, and all prompt/output history is logged immutably for audit trails.

**Q: What blockchains does K≈çbo support?**  
A: Currently Ethereum, Polygon, Base, and Arbitrum, with additional chains added via DAO governance proposals.

**Q: How do remix battles work?**  
A: Weekly themed competitions where creators remix existing NFTs or prompts. Community votes determine winners, with prize pools distributed via smart contracts. All derivatives maintain provenance links to parent NFTs.

**Q: What is the $KOBO token used for?**  
A: Governance voting, battle entry fees, prompt marketplace transactions, and platform fee discounts. Token holders participate in DAO decisions about new features, AI engine integrations, and treasury allocation.
