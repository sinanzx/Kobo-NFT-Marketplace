## Community Engagement Guidelines

### Response Time Targets
- Twitter mentions: < 2 hours during business hours
- Discord questions: < 30 minutes
- Instagram comments: < 4 hours
- Email: < 24 hours

### Tone of Voice
- **Friendly**: Approachable, not corporate
- **Knowledgeable**: Expert but not condescending
- **Enthusiastic**: Genuine excitement about creator success
- **Transparent**: Honest about challenges and roadmap

### Crisis Communication
- Acknowledge issues immediately
- Provide timeline for resolution
- Update every 2-4 hours until resolved
- Post-mortem after major incidents
