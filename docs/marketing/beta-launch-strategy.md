# Kōbo Beta Launch Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Objective**: Launch Kōbo beta to 500 power users from NFT/AI communities, validate product-market fit, gather feedback, and build launch momentum for mainnet.

**Timeline**: 8 weeks from beta launch to mainnet  
**Target Users**: 500 beta testers (Week 1-4), expand to 2,000 (Week 5-8)  
**Success Metrics**: 70% weekly active users, 5,000+ NFTs minted, 4.5+ NPS score

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Beta (Weeks -2 to 0)

### Week -2: Waitlist Building

**Goal**: Generate 2,000+ waitlist signups

**Tactics**
- Launch landing page with beta signup form
- Twitter/X announcement thread with demo video
- Discord server opening with exclusive roles for early joiners
- Bluesky technical deep-dive posts
- Instagram teaser campaign
- Partnerships with NFT influencers for waitlist promotion

**Waitlist Incentives**
- Early access guaranteed for first 500 signups
- Exclusive "Genesis Beta Tester" NFT airdrop
- Lifetime 50% discount on platform fees
- Priority access to future features
- Voting power in first DAO proposals

**Content Calendar**
- **Monday**: Waitlist announcement with demo video
- **Wednesday**: Feature spotlight (multi-modal AI)
- **Friday**: Influencer partnership announcement
- **Weekend**: Community Q&A in Discord

---

### Week -1: Beta Tester Selection

**Goal**: Curate 500 high-quality beta testers

**Selection Criteria**
- **NFT Creators** (40%): Active minters on OpenSea, Foundation, etc.
- **AI Artists** (30%): Users of Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion
- **Web3 Developers** (15%): Smart contract experience, dApp builders
- **Community Leaders** (10%): Discord mods, Twitter influencers
- **Diverse Backgrounds** (5%): Ensure geographic, demographic diversity

**Outreach Strategy**
- Email invitations to selected waitlist members
- Direct Twitter DMs to target creators
- Discord announcements in partner communities
- Personalized video invitations for top 50 influencers

**Beta Tester Onboarding Kit**
- Welcome email with access instructions
- Video tutorial: "Your First 5 Minutes on Kōbo"
- Discord invite with exclusive #beta-testers channel
- Feedback form and bug reporting guide
- $50 worth of testnet ETH for gas-free testing

## Beta Feedback Loop

### Feedback Collection Methods

**In-App Surveys**
- Post-mint: "How was your minting experience?" (1-5 stars)
- Weekly: "What feature should we prioritize?" (multiple choice)
- Monthly: Comprehensive NPS survey

**Discord Feedback**
- Dedicated #feedback channel
- Weekly "Feedback Friday" voice calls
- Emoji reactions for quick sentiment

**User Interviews**
- 1-on-1 calls with 20 power users per week
- 30-minute structured interviews
- $50 compensation in $KOBO tokens

**Bug Reporting**
- #bug-reports Discord channel
- In-app bug report button
- Bounty program: $10-$100 per valid bug (severity-based)

**Analytics Tracking**
- Mixpanel for user behavior
- Hotjar for session recordings
- Custom dashboard for key metrics

---

### Feedback Prioritization Framework

**P0 - Critical (Fix within 24 hours)**
- Security vulnerabilities
- Data loss issues
- Complete feature breakage
- Smart contract bugs

**P1 - High (Fix within 1 week)**
- Major UX friction
- Performance issues
- Copyright compliance failures
- Payment/transaction errors

**P2 - Medium (Fix within 2 weeks)**
- Minor UX improvements
- Feature enhancements
- Visual bugs
- Edge case handling

**P3 - Low (Backlog)**
- Nice-to-have features
- Minor visual polish
- Documentation improvements
- Future integrations

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

**Risk**: Smart contract vulnerabilities  
**Mitigation**: 
- Multiple security audits (CertiK, OpenZeppelin)
- Bug bounty program ($50K pool)
- Gradual rollout with transaction limits
- Emergency pause functionality

**Risk**: IPFS storage failures  
**Mitigation**:
- Multiple pinning services (Pinata, NFT.Storage, Filebase)
- Redundant storage across providers
- Automated health checks
- Backup to Arweave for critical assets

**Risk**: AI API rate limits/downtime  
**Mitigation**:
- Multiple AI engine providers per modality
- Fallback engines configured
- Queue system for high-demand periods
- Clear user communication on delays

---

### Community Risks

**Risk**: Low engagement/retention  
**Mitigation**:
- Daily engagement campaigns
- Gamification (badges, leaderboards)
- Regular contests with prizes
- Personalized onboarding

**Risk**: Toxic community behavior  
**Mitigation**:
- Clear community guidelines
- Active moderation team
- Reporting and blocking features
- Positive reinforcement programs

**Risk**: Influencer backlash  
**Mitigation**:
- Transparent communication
- Early influencer involvement
- Responsive to feedback
- Crisis communication plan

---

### Legal Risks

**Risk**: Copyright infringement claims  
**Mitigation**:
- Robust pre-check scanning
- Clear TOS and user agreements
- DMCA takedown process
- Legal counsel on retainer

**Risk**: Regulatory compliance (securities)  
**Mitigation**:
- Legal review of $KOBO tokenomics
- Avoid investment language
- Utility-focused messaging
- Jurisdiction-specific compliance
