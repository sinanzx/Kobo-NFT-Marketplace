# NFT Battles User Guide

## What are NFT Battles?

NFT Battles are competitive creative challenges where you can pit your NFTs against others to earn rewards, climb leaderboards, and showcase your creativity. Battles combine artistic skill, community voting, and strategic gameplay.

## Getting Started

### Viewing Active Battles

1. **From Homepage**: Scroll to the "NFT Battles" showcase section to see the top 3 active battles
2. **From Navigation**: Click "Battles" in the main navigation menu
3. **From Gallery**: Click the "Battle" button on any NFT card to enter it into a battle

### Battle Information

Each battle card displays:
- **Battle Type**: The category or theme of the battle
- **Prize Pool**: Total rewards available to winners
- **Title**: Name of the battle
- **Description**: Battle rules and requirements
- **Participants**: Current vs. maximum participants
- **Status**: Active, Voting, or Ended

## Joining a Battle

### Prerequisites
- Connected wallet
- At least one minted NFT in your collection
- NFT must meet battle requirements (if any)

### Steps to Join

1. Navigate to the Battles page
2. Browse available battles
3. Click "Join Battle" on your chosen battle
4. Select the NFT you want to enter
5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
6. Wait for blockchain confirmation

### Battle Entry Requirements

Different battles may have specific requirements:
- **Theme Battles**: NFT must match the theme
- **Trait Battles**: NFT must have specific traits
- **Open Battles**: Any NFT can participate
- **Skill Battles**: Based on creation method or complexity

## Battle Types

### 1. Theme Battles
- Specific creative theme or concept
- Judged on theme interpretation
- Examples: "Cyberpunk Dreams", "Nature's Fury", "Abstract Emotions"

### 2. Trait Battles
- Focus on specific NFT traits
- Rarity or uniqueness of traits matters
- Examples: "Rarest Background", "Most Dynamic Traits"

### 3. Community Battles
- Community voting determines winners
- Social engagement matters
- Share your entry to gain votes

### 4. Timed Battles
- Limited time to create and submit
- Tests speed and creativity
- Quick turnaround challenges

## Battle Phases

### Phase 1: Entry Period
- Duration: Varies by battle (typically 24-72 hours)
- Submit your NFT
- View other participants
- Prepare your strategy

### Phase 2: Voting Period
- Duration: Typically 24-48 hours
- Community votes on entries
- Share your entry for more votes
- Track your position on leaderboard

### Phase 3: Results
- Winners announced
- Prizes distributed automatically
- Battle stats finalized
- Leaderboard updated

## Voting System

### How to Vote
1. Navigate to an active battle in voting phase
2. Browse all entries
3. Click on entries to view details
4. Cast your vote for your favorite
5. You can vote once per battle

### Voting Criteria
Consider these factors when voting:
- **Creativity**: Originality and innovation
- **Theme Adherence**: How well it matches the battle theme
- **Artistic Quality**: Visual appeal and execution
- **Uniqueness**: Stands out from other entries

## Prizes and Rewards

## Battle Rules and Guidelines

### Fair Play
- One entry per wallet per battle
- No vote manipulation or bot usage
- Original creations only
- Follow community guidelines

### Prohibited Actions

- Creating multiple accounts
- Vote buying or trading

### Consequences
Violations may result in:
- Entry disqualification
- Prize forfeiture
- Account suspension
- Permanent ban (severe cases)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I withdraw my entry?
Yes, during the entry period only. Once voting starts, entries are locked.

### What happens if I don't win?
You still earn participation points and experience. Every battle helps you improve!

### Can I enter multiple battles simultaneously?
Yes, you can enter as many battles as you want with different NFTs.

### How are ties handled?
In case of tied votes, the entry submitted first wins the higher position.

### Can I vote for my own entry?
No, you cannot vote for your own entries to ensure fairness.

### What if a battle doesn't fill up?
Battles with minimum participant requirements may be cancelled and entry fees refunded.

### Are there entry fees?
Some battles may have small entry fees that contribute to the prize pool. This is clearly indicated.

### Can I edit my entry after submission?
No, entries are final once submitted. Choose carefully!
