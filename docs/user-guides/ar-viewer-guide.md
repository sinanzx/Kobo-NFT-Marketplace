# AR/VR Viewer User Guide

## What is the AR/VR Viewer?

The AR/VR Viewer allows you to experience your NFTs in immersive augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) environments. View your digital art in your physical space, explore virtual galleries, or step into fully immersive VR experiences.

## Supported Platforms

### Mobile AR
- **iOS**: iPhone 6S and newer (iOS 11+)
- **Android**: ARCore-compatible devices (Android 7.0+)
- **Features**: Place NFTs in your real environment using your phone camera

### Web-Based Viewer
- **Desktop**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
- **Mobile**: Mobile browsers with WebGL support
- **Features**: 3D gallery view, interactive controls, no installation required

### VR Headsets
- **Meta Quest 2/3**: Native support
- **HTC Vive**: SteamVR compatible
- **Valve Index**: Full support
- **PlayStation VR**: Coming soon
- **Features**: Full immersive VR gallery experience

## Getting Started

### Accessing the AR Viewer

1. **From Homepage**: Click "Try AR Demo" in the AR/VR Preview section
2. **From Navigation**: Click "AR Viewer" in the main menu
3. **From Gallery**: Click the AR icon on any NFT card
4. **From NFT Details**: Click "View in AR" button

### First-Time Setup

#### Mobile AR Setup
1. Grant camera permissions when prompted
2. Allow motion sensor access
3. Find a well-lit area with a flat surface
4. Point camera at the surface to detect plane
5. Tap to place your NFT

#### VR Headset Setup
1. Install the KoboNFT VR app from your headset's store
2. Connect your wallet via QR code
3. Grant necessary permissions
4. Complete the VR tutorial
5. Access your NFT collection

## Mobile AR Features

### Placing NFTs in Your Space

**Step-by-Step**
1. Open AR viewer for your chosen NFT
2. Move your phone slowly to scan the environment
3. White dots appear when a surface is detected
4. Tap where you want to place the NFT
5. NFT appears in your space at real-world scale

## Troubleshooting

### AR Issues

**Can't Detect Surfaces**
- Ensure good lighting
- Move phone slowly
- Try different surface
- Clean camera lens
- Restart app

**NFT Not Appearing**
- Check internet connection
- Reload the NFT
- Clear app cache
- Update app
- Restart device

**Tracking Lost**
- Return to original position
- Improve lighting
- Reduce movement
- Avoid reflective surfaces
- Recalibrate

### Web Viewer Issues

**Slow Performance**
- Lower quality settings
- Close other tabs
- Update graphics drivers
- Use hardware acceleration
- Try different browser

**NFT Not Loading**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page
- Clear browser cache
- Disable ad blockers
- Try incognito mode

**Controls Not Working**
- Check browser compatibility
- Enable JavaScript
- Disable browser extensions
- Try different input device
- Restart browser

### VR Issues

**Headset Not Connecting**
- Check WiFi connection
- Restart headset
- Update firmware
- Reinstall app
- Check account connection

**Performance Issues**
- Lower graphics settings
- Close background apps
- Clear cache
- Restart headset
- Check available storage
